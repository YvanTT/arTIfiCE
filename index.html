arTIfiCE is a jailbreak for TI CE calculators.
It brings back ASM programs and games!

Compatibility

arTIfiCE v2 works on all CE calculators, from OS 5.3 to 5.8.3.
Note: it is not needed on OS 5.4 or earlier!
  • TI-83 Premium CE (Edition Python)
  • TI-84 Plus CE (Python)
  • TI-84 Plus CE-T (Python Edition)
The TI-82 Advanced Edition Python is also compatible (from OS 5.6.3 to 5.6.5).
